Why is there a need to manage Chronic Diseases?

  • The long-term effects may be difficult to predict.
  • While some conditions cause few problems, others cause only episodic problems or symptoms that can be controlled with medication but need regular monitoring. In some cases, chronic disease may severely limit a person’s ability to work, go to school, or take care of routine needs.
